Leo Messi

Greatest of all time

Career highlights:

  • Born June 1987 in Rosario, Argentina.
  • Plays as a forward for Spanish club FC Barcelona and the Argentina national team.
  • Messi is the only player in history to win five FIFA Ballon d'Or awards, four of which he won consecutively, and a record-tying four European Golden Shoes.
  • He has scored over 500 senior career goals for club and country.
